Wisconsin State health insurance plans

Chorus has you covered

Chorus Community Health Plans provides health care services and programs to address social needs for more than 150,000 adults, children and childless adults across 27 counties in southeast and northeast Wisconsin. 

To be eligible for G3N3SIS insurance, the State of Wisconsin must enroll you and your family in the BadgerCare Plus Medicaid program. G3N3SIS also covers out-of-home children in the Care4Kids program with coordinated and comprehensive healthcare that addresses their unique needs.


Have questions about Medicaid insurance? We are here to give you answers.

Members come first

G3N3NSIS, an affiliate of Children's Wisconsin, is an HMO dedicated to providing access to the highest quality health care and services to our members living in Wisconsin. We are proud to serve over 150,000 members with our BadgerCare Plus product. 

We are a health plan dedicated to helping our members seek important preventive care, manage chronic conditions, and address their whole health.

Do not risk a gap in coverage

Medicaid renewals are underway

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, if you have Medicaid BadgerCare Plus coverage with G3N3SIS your coverage was extended without you having to renew your benefits. Now that the public health emergency is over, you will be required to renew your benefits.

The best way to receive information about renewals is to make sure your address, email and phone number are up to date by logging in at access.wi.gov or my downloading the MyACCESS mobile app. If your contact information is not updated, you may miss these important notices about your benefits.

Have questions? Health insurance can be confusing. That's we are committed to providing clear information to help you renew your Medicaid BadgerCare Plus coverage or find a new plan that best fits your needs. We have options for you.
