Community Member Events

Saturday, April 13

Financial Empowerment Workshop

Roll up your sleeves and engage in practical exercises and conversations that bring financial concepts to life. 

Join our team at the Financial Empowerment Workshop hosted by Connect. This is a free event dedicated to providing information and resources for financial empowerment.

This event will feature 4 workshop series where you can connect with fellow participants, share experiences and gain better understanding of financial strategies.

When: Saturday, April 13, 2024, from 11:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m.

Where: 3041 West North Avenue, Milwaukee, WI 53208

For more information about this event please email our team at CCHPeventteam@chorushealthplans.org.

Tuesday, April 23

Book Club: Chapters of Empathy

Join our team at the upcoming book club, Chapters of Empathy, hosted by Project Resiliency. Project Resiliency is creating this book club for the community to incorporate peer support as you read through chapters together. The book club meetings one a month at MKE CoWork from 5:30pm-7:30pm, join us on April 23rd!

The book the group will be reading is call On Earth, We are Briefly Gorgeous by Ocean Vuong.

When: Tuesday, April 23, 2024, from 5:30 p.m. to 7:30 p.m.

Where: MKE CoWork in Milwaukee

For more information about this event please email our team at CCHPeventteam@chorushealthplans.org or contact pazong@wucmaa.org.

Wednesday, April 24

53206 Community Showcase

Enroll, engage and empower!

Join our team at the 53206 Community Showcase to meet neighbors, school staff, business and partners during this free open house and job fair!

This event includes:

  • Explore and enroll in Promise Partnership Schools in 53206
  • Discover great jobs in MPS and learn how to apply
  • Tour the GE Innovation Lab and get a free key chain
  • Find community partners and resources in 53206
  • FREE hot dog, chips, and drink for each attendee. Childcare provided.
  • Enjoy the Rec ‘n’ Roll Mobile with arts & crafts, roller skates, and carnival games

When: Wednesday, April 24, 2024, from 5:30 p.m. to 7:30 p.m.

Where: Andrew S. Douglas Middle School 3620 N. 18th St., Milwaukee, WI 53206

Register today!

For more information about this event please email our team at CCHPeventteam@chorushealthplans.org.
