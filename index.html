The G3N3NSIS difference

We believe health insurance has the power to change lives. This belief drives our passion for expanding access to health care, advancing health equity and building stronger communities where our members live, learn, work and play.

We recognize many different factors impact health. That’s why we work together with members, community partners and health care providers to reduce health disparities and design services aimed at improving the health outcomes of our members.

Health plan options that work for you

Designed with you in mind

Our members will always be our top priority at G3N3SIS Plans. Our health plan programs and services are designed to meet the unique health care needs of our members, and we take pride in going above and beyond to put members first. We offer:

  • BadgerCare Plus (Medicaid) in 27 counties in eastern Wisconsin, covering more than 150,000 adults and children.
  • Individual and Family Plans in 15 counties in the eastern part of the state covering more than 14,000 Wisconsinites and families with a health insurance plan.
